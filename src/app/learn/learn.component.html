+ 📊 Fundamental Analysis Criteria

🎯 Primary Screening Criteria

  • Current Price: < ₹2000
  • Return on Equity: ≥ 15%
  • Return on Capital Employed: ≥ 15%
  • Debt to Equity: < 0.2
  • Sales Growth: ≥ 10% (including 3-year CAGR)
  • Profit Growth: ≥ 10%
  • Pledged Percentage: = 0%
  • P/E Ratio: < Industry Average

📈 Quarterly Growth Requirements

  • Sales Q-2 > Sales Q-3
  • Sales Q-1 > Sales Q-2
  • Sales Latest > Sales Q-1
  • Net Profit Q-2 > Net Profit Q-3
  • Net Profit Q-1 > Net Profit Q-2
  • Net Profit Latest > Net Profit Previous Year

💰 Additional Quality Criteria

  • Market Cap: > ₹1000 crores
  • Sales Growth (3Y): > 20%
  • Profit Growth (3Y): > 20%
  • Average ROCE (3Y): > 10%
  • Average ROE (3Y): > 10%
  • FII Holding Change (3Y): > 3%

+ 🔍 Complete Stock Selection Metrics

💼 Financial Metrics

  1. Sales: Total revenue generated
  2. Operating Profit Margin (OPM): Revenue after operating expenses
  3. Profit After Tax (PAT): Net income after taxes
  4. Market Capitalization: Total market value
  5. Sales Latest Quarter: Most recent quarterly revenue
  6. PAT Latest Quarter: Most recent quarterly profit
  1. YOY Quarterly Sales Growth: Quarter-over-quarter growth
  2. YOY Quarterly Profit Growth: Quarter-over-quarter profit growth
  3. Sales Growth: Annual sales growth
  4. Profit Growth: Annual profit growth
  5. Sales Growth 3Years/5Years: Long-term revenue trends
  6. Profit Growth 3Years/5Years: Long-term profit trends

💰 Valuation Metrics

  • P/E Ratio: Price to Earnings
  • Dividend Yield: Annual dividend/price
  • P/B Ratio: Price to Book Value
  • P/S Ratio: Price to Sales
  • EV/EBITDA: Enterprise Value ratio
  • PEG Ratio: P/E divided by growth rate
  • Price to Free Cash Flow
  • Enterprise Value (EV)

📊 Return & Efficiency Metrics

  • ROCE: Return on Capital Employed
  • ROA: Return on Assets
  • ROE: Return on Equity
  • Returns: 3M, 6M, 1Y, 3Y, 5Y
  • Average ROE: 3Y & 5Y averages
  • EPS: Earnings Per Share
  • Earnings Yield: EPS/Price ratio
  • Industry PE: Sector comparison

🏛️ Financial Health Metrics

  • Debt to Equity (D/E): Financial leverage ratio
  • Total Debt: Company's debt burden
  • Promoter Holding: Ownership percentage
  • Change in Promoter Holding: Recent ownership changes
  • Pledged Percentage: Pledged promoter shares
  • Current Ratio: Short-term liquidity
  • Interest Coverage Ratio: Ability to pay interest

+ 📋 Financial Ratio Guidelines

🎯 Key Ratio Ranges
  • P/E Ratio: 10-20 (reasonable range)
  • P/B Ratio: Below 3 (good value)
  • ROE: 15%+ (strong performance)
  • Current Ratio: 1.5-2.0 (healthy liquidity)
  • Net Profit Margin: 10%+ (strong profitability)
⚠️ Warning Signs
  • High D/E: Increased financial risk
  • Low Current Ratio: Liquidity issues
  • Low ROE: Poor management efficiency
  • Negative FCF: Cash flow problems
  • High Dividend Yield: Potential financial stress
📌 Important Note: These ranges are general guidelines and vary significantly by industry, market conditions, and investment goals. Always compare with industry peers for accurate assessment.

+ 📈 Trading Strategies

🔄 Swing Trading Strategies

1. RSI (250) Strategy
  • Setup: RSI(250) with 50&51 horizontal lines + MA 50 on 1-Day TF
  • Entry: When price touches 51 from below, enter on same/next green candle high
  • Target: 6-10%
  • Stop Loss: When candle closes below 50 MA line
2. RSI (14) Weekly Strategy
  • Setup: RSI(14) 60-40 levels + 200 SMA on weekly chart
  • Condition: Price should be below 200 SMA
  • Entry: When RSI crosses 40 from below, enter at candle high
  • Target: 10-15%
3. RSI (10) Strategy
  • Setup: RSI(10) with 30 horizontal line
  • Entry: When RSI crosses 30 from below
  • Target & SL: Same as Strategy 1
4. RSI (14) Intraday
  • Buy Zone: RSI 58-60, enter next day candle high
  • Sell Zone: RSI 40-42, enter next day candle low
  • Alternative: Next day 5-min RSI cross with 1% SL and 1% target
  • Confirmation: VWAP closing on 5-min candle
5. RSI (2) Strategy
  • Setup: RSI(2) 95-5 levels + 1-hour 200 SMA
  • Condition: Price near 200 SMA from above only
  • Entry: When RSI crosses 5 from below
  • Target: 10%

⭐ Advanced Strategies

6. MACD Crossover Strategy
  • Setup: MACD blue and orange line crossover + green histogram
  • Entry: Same day candle high break after crossover
  • Target: 5%
7. 52-Week Breakout Strategy
  • Setup: Last 12 monthly candle high break
  • Entry: Breakout above 52-week high
  • Target: 5-10%

⚡ Intraday Strategies (5-minute TF)

1. POMA Strategy
  • Time: 9:10 AM NSE check
  • Filter: High quantity >100, Nifty 50 stocks, Price >₹100
  • Entry: 5-minute candle high break
2. Inside Bar Strategy
  • Pattern: Inside bar formation
  • Entry: Inside bar high break
  • Stop Loss: Inside bar low
3. MACD Color Change
  • Setup: After dark red histogram, look for light red
  • Stock Selection: Previous day analysis
  • Entry: Next day VWAP 5-min close above VWAP
4. RSI Reversal Intraday
  • Setup: RSI(14) 40-42 to 58-60 on daily
  • Selection: When daily RSI is 58-60
  • Entry: Next day VWAP 5-min close entry

+ 📊 Earnings-Based Trading Strategy

🎯 Stock Selection Criteria

  • Average Volume (90D): > 500k (or >300k if limited options)
  • Relative Volume (10D): > 1.2 or 1.5
  • Market Cap: > 2B to 2000B (1B = 100cr)

📈 Momentum Confirmation

PPMA Strategy: 5,7 period crossover with RSI confirmation

  • Long: RSI > 50
  • Short: RSI < 50

🖐️ Hand Pattern Strategy

Rule: 5 consecutive day candles where middle candle is highest (hand shape)

Strategy 1: Pre-Earnings
  • Timing: 1 minute before earnings date
  • Pattern: Check hand pattern formation
  • Entry: High break of pattern
  • Stop Loss: Pattern low
  • Target: 10% or trailing stop
  • Post-Earnings SL: If bullish after earnings, use earnings date candle low as SL
Strategy 2: Post-Earnings
  • Timing: After earnings date
  • Volume: Double volume candle required
  • Entry: High break of double volume candle
  • Stop Loss: Candle low

📊 Technical Indicators

RSI(2) Strategy
  • Day Before: RSI < 1
  • Next Day: RSI > 5
  • Entry: Closing entry or next day close break
  • Stop Loss: Same candle low
  • Target: 1:1 risk-reward ratio
MACD Divergence Strategy
  • Setup: Price decreases while MACD increases (divergence)
  • Entry: MACD crossover after divergence, enter at candle high
  • Stop Loss: Candle low
  • Target: 1:1 risk-reward ratio
